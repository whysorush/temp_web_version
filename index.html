FEW MARQUEE TRANSACTIONS

  • The client was seeking a refinancing opportunity to give exit to a PSU bank as principal repayment was about to trigger.
  • We successfully executed the deal through an NBFC, benefiting them with extended tenure along with enhancement for purchase of land parcel for developing large township.

Deal Details:- Structured Debt for a SRA project in Mumbai [ ₹780 crores ]

  • Structured 3 years LC confirmation & discounting, under approval route, using credit wrap from Insurance to enhance capacity of two banks involved in taking out risk of Issuing Bank based in Bangladesh.
  • The Beneficiary was based in Europe. This enabled the Client to timely set up a greenfield HFO based Power Plant.

Deal Details:- Deal Details: Trade Finance for Conglomerate in Bangladesh [Euros 32.5 Million ]

  • The client was seeking a rate reduction from an NBFC and further funds to construct new Warehouse. But the lease lock-in Tenure was only 2 years. We successfully executed the deal through a Private Bank.
  • Releasing Promoter's Personal Guarantee and self-occupied residential property was value added service given by Growthally.

Deal Details: Construction Finance cum LRD Finance for an Integrated Logistic Park at Nagpur [₹133cr]

  • The client was seeking for takeover of the existing limits due to higher interest rate. We shifted their limits to PSU bank with enhanced working capital limits and reduced the ROI to 8.35% from 14.80% We also gave financial advisory to client in takeover of an old business from another promoter.
  • Assisted in release of other family member's Personal Guarantees and cross collateralization of Inter Co. properties.

Deal Details: For Takeover cum Loan Enhancement for Stainless Steel Mfg. Co. in Ahmedabad, having Revenue of Rs.250 Crs [ ₹34 crores ]

  • The Developer was unable to meet the repayment commitment and was declared as NPA by its PSU Lender. The Trustees had infused Capex of Rs.15 Crs from own sources and wanted cash out on same.
  • We structured the deal from Bank in such a way that 100% of the money infused by the Trustees in form of Capex was paid back to them.

Deal Details: Cash-out to Trustees for a Medical College and Hospital in Palakkad [ ₹15 crores ]

  • We structured a takeover wherein the loan amount was higher than the size of existing balance sheet of borrower. Assisted in release of other family member's Personal Guarantees and cross collateralization of Inter Co. properties.
  • We arranged the fund at competitive ROI without any External Credit Rating.

Deal Details: Structured Debt for a Chemical & Pharma player in Ankleshwar [ ₹20 crores ]
